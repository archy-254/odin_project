Description.

East African fragrant rice dish made by cooking rice with some type of meat or vegetables, garlic, ginger, and spices. It is usually served with Kachumbari (a tomato and onion salad).

Ingredients.

  • Cumin seeds
  • cloves
  • tomato paste
  • meat
  • ginger
  • garlic

Steps.

  1. Heat the oil in a sufuria over medium-high heat.
  2. Add the onions to the pan and cook, until they are lightly browned.
  3. Add the cumin, cinnamon, cardamom, and garlic to the pan and stir to combine.
  4. Season your chicken pieces with salt and pepper. .
  5. Add rice to the pan, and stir to combine.
  6. Add salt and water.
